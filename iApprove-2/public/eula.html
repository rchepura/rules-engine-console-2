Effective Date: November 13, 2015

The Estée Lauder Companies Inc. ("Estée Lauder”) is responsible for the processing of your personal data obtained through the MAC IDEA mobile app (the "App” or "MAC IDEA”). This Online Privacy Notice describes the types of information Estée Lauder obtains through the App, how the company uses the information and with whom Estée Lauder shares the information.

What information Estée Lauder processes

Through the App, Estée Lauder processes personal data, such as your name, employee ID, region and job title. In addition, content you provide through the App (such as photographs, articles, comments and postings) may contain personal data, which will be available through MAC IDEA for your use and the use of other Estée Lauder employees. Estée Lauder may collect certain information on the App through automated means. The information Estée Lauder obtains via automated means includes device type, device identifiers and information about your interactions with the App.

How Estée Lauder uses your information

MAC IDEA is a mobile app that allows full-time employees to share postings, photographs, articles, comments and experiences that support their career development. Estée Lauder uses the information obtained through to App to facilitate this sharing, authenticate your eligibility to use the App, populate basic information in your profile page on the App, identify and leverage resources, communicate with employees, assign projects and duties, analyze your use of the App, update and improve the App, and comply with applicable legal requirements and company policies, including the Code of Conduct.

Sharing of information

Your personal data may be disclosed to agents, contractors and other third-party service providers. Estée Lauder requires these service providers to provide appropriate data security and confidentiality for your personal data. Your personal data also may be disclosed to other Estée Lauder employees and Estée Lauder’s subsidiaries and affiliates worldwide.

Your options

On the App, you may update certain of your personal data, including the personal data on your profile page and you may remove any content you upload through the App, such as photographs and comments

Data security

Estée Lauder maintains appropriate administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to protect employee personal data against accidental, unlawful or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, disclosure or access or use, and other unlawful forms of processing.
